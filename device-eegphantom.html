EEG Phantom

A Rhythmlink LLC Product

The custom PCB I designed in KiCad and milled on our Bantim milling machine intended to step down the voltage of our brain waves output.

Utilizing WaveForms to read back our two phantom electrodes during an early prototype of the device. The screen displays the read brain waves.

Displaying read analog data from the output electrodes and displaying it using the Pyplot library graping it over the course of time.

What is the goal of the EEG Phantom?

The EEG Phantom is a tool that simulates a human scalp in both material and brain activity in order to create a product that would allow Rhythmlink to test their own EEG products on our predictable modifiable Phantom as opposed to an unpredictable human subject.
