Proelium

Proelium is a multiplayer survival sandbox I developed over the course of 3 years. Inspired by my favorite games like Terraria & Minecraft, Proelium embodied my idea of an ideal game. I developed everything myself, including artwork, animations, sound design, coding, and networking.

Watch a Trailer

How was it developed?

Proelium was developed in the Unity Engine (2019 Edition) with external tools such as Aseperite (for artwork) and Shotcut (for sound editing). Additionally it utilized external packages like Text Mesh Pro and Photon's networking API and systems as the backbone for networking.
