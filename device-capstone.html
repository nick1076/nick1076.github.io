Vinyl Sticker

I designed a vinyl sticker as my first project of the class. I utilized Silhouette Studio and an image I found online in order to make it. I chose to do an off-suite 7 2 poker hand as my sticker which I would eventually put on my cardboard box.

Pen

I began with just a wooden block and using a bandsaw, lathe, and pen press was able to turn it into a professional looking wooden pen, both sanded and sealed in addition.

Shadowbox

Usin Corel Draw and online images as well as my own design skills I designed a car-themed shadowbox, a shadowbox being a multilayer box with light coming from the back through the layers. I designed mine to be a car showroom with a city backdrop, I even chose to include some of my lighting above the cars in further up layers of the box as opposed to just having them in the furthest layer.

Pencil

In addition to a wooden pen, I also made a wooden pencil. Using an identical process excluding the bandsaw, I used a single block of wood, turning it via the lathe, and then pressed it using the provided kit to make a professional looking wooden pencil.
