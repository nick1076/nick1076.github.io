Celestial Shootout

2023

This gif displays the combo system in full effect. Consecutive kills by the player on any enemy type will increase the current combo, and once it reaches five or any combo following five, it increases the player's health by one health point. Missing any shot will reset this combo, but one a room is cleared the player can freely shoot without losing their combo until they move on to the next room.

The player in this gif is fighting off a set of more challenging enemies, enemies which specifically target and go after the player. These are found in later levels of the game but seen here are killed consecutively giving the player a combo of two. You can also see the player check their watch toward the end of the gif, which indicates to the player their current health points as small red cubes.

Lastly, the player is pictured fighting the hardest enemy in the game. This enemy, inspired by Half-Life 2's Combine Elite Soldier in terms of design & look is a combination of the yellow impulse enemy seen in gif two and a new enemy type all together. It not only launches itself at the player to deal melee damage, but it also shoots a damaging beam directly at the player too at the same time.

What is Celestial Shootout?

Celestial Shootout is a game I designed start to finish entirely in one week at a George Mason University game development course. I centered the game design entirely around a single mechanic: gun knockback. Being in a zero gravity environment, the player must use their weapon as a means of moving around the level while also using the gun to fight off enemies. It is a challenging arcade shooter about clearing rooms of enemies while also managing your momentum and keeping control of your player.
