What is Cartoon Cat?

Cartoon Cat was an indie horror I developed solo based on the popular horror figure Cartoon Cat by Trevor Henderson. It was also my first ever official release of a game, and even with no marketing as of writing this it has over 850 downloads and a long list of YouTube playthroughs and comments on it's itch.io page.

Playthroughs & Ratings

Cartoon Cat hit the itch.io store as a free indie horror with zero marketing, but even so against the odds, it met immediete attention from players on the platform, including comments, reviews, walk throughs, and many high ratings. Gameplay Video

This video pictures the early development of the maze portion of the game in which a NavMesh Agent (Cartoon Cat) hunts down the player. Utilizing Unity's NavMesh system I placed a set of points at each turn in the maze and attached to those intersection points each of the following points for each direction. When not having the player in direct sight, Cartoon Cat will pick at random proceeding points once it reaches an intersection. If the player is in view, it will strictly pursue the player until the player is out of sight of Cartoon Cat for 10 seconds, at which point it returns to it's point by point intersection pathfinding, as pictured in the video (Of course with no walls in the video Cartoon Cat is mainly utlizing the chase system as opposed to the point by point intersection system).
