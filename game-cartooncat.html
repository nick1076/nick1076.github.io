Cartoon Cat

2021

Here the player is pictured outside holding their camera at the start of the game, tasks are seen in the top right and an eye icon for a readable newspaper can be seen on the hood of the police car.

The player walks down the stairs to enter the bunker which they ultimately stay in for the remained of the game, the flashlight is in use here and the item system becomes crucial later on.

Pictured here the player stands by a set of lit up red boards holding the hammer item in preperation to breakdown the boards to enter a previously blocked off room to progress the story.

What is Cartoon Cat?

Cartoon Cat was an indie horror I developed solo based on the popular horror figure Cartoon Cat by Trevor Henderson. It was also my first ever official release of a game, and even with no marketing as of writing this it has over 850 downloads and a long list of YouTube playthroughs and comments on it's itch.io page.

Playthroughs & Ratings

Cartoon Cat hit the itch.io store as a free indie horror with zero marketing, but even so against the odds, it met immediete attention from players on the platform, including comments, reviews, walk throughs, and many high ratings.
